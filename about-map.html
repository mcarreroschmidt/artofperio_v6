Least Complicated
Rt. 80W (exit 27 Somerville) -> Rt. 206S (to Chester, Larison's Turkey Farm on your right) turn LEFT at light -> Rt. 513 (Main St.) through Chester and go across next traffic light. The office is the first driveway on your right. It is a beige and stone building with large letters stating CROSSROADS BUILDING and a blue and orange sign underneath stating Concentra (formerly IMCC).

Most Direct
Rt. 80W -> Rt. 287S -> Rt. 10W (past County College of Morris on your left) take jughandle to turn left -> 513S also called Dover-Chester Rd. (past CCM entrance on your left). At approximately 8 miles there will be a telephone pole tree farm on your left. We are the next driveway on your left just before the light. It is a beige and stone building with large letters stating CROSSROADS BUILDING and a blue and orange sign underneath stating Concentra (formerly IMCC).

From Rt. 78 or 287
Rt. 78W -> Rt. 287N (exit 22B Pluckemin) -> Rt. 202/206N which then splits -> Rt. 206N to the next town which is Chester. Right at the traffic light -> Rt. 513 (Main St.) through Chester to the next light. Go through traffic light and make a right into the first driveway. It is a beige and stone building with large letters stating CROSSROADS BUILDING and a blue and orange sign underneath stating Concentra (formerly IMCC).
