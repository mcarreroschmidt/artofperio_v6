The second visit to our office will include a private discussion between you and Dr. Goteiner regarding the findings from your initial examination. Among the points covered will be:

  • An explanation of the diagnosis
  • A recommended plan of treatment, the number of appointments, and fee
  • Treatment alternatives

We will keep your dentist fully informed of your treatment plan and progress.

Please feel free to ask the doctor and staff any questions about any aspect of your treatment. It is important that you understand and feel comfortable about the treatment plan.
