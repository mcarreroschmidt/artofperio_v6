Reprinted from the Observer-Tribune and the Mt. Olive Chronicle.

WASHINGTON TWP. – Nicole Mauro, who graduated in June from West Morris Central High School and Lily Yengle, a June graduate of Mendham High School, were among the recipients of an annual award presented by a Chester dentist and his wife.

Chester dentist David Goteiner and his wife, Carrie, have been honoring students in the area since 1985.
The Community Service Award is sponsored by Dr. David Goteiner, and his wife, Carrie.

Award winners are selected based on recommendations from guidance counselors at their schools. One of the selection criteria is that the student be involved in selfless acts of community service, said a statement.

Each recipient will receive a bond. Other winners were Cynthia Cheng, Hackettstown High School; Alexandra Moriyon, Mount Olive High School; Nicole Dussault, Randolph High School; and Shanna Irwin, Roxbury High School.
